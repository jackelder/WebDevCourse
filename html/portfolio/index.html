Get to Know Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Minnesota with a Masters of Science in Chemistry. I have also trained and worked as a chemist at the University of Puget Sound and the Infectious Disease Research Institute in Seattle, Washington.

I am currently pursuing a transition into a career in software development.

Past Experiences

While at the University of Puget Sound, I performed two summers of undergraduate research and a senior thesis in organic synthesis. I also worked as a Peer Advisor to assist first-year students in their transition to college. I have been tutoring general and organic chemisty for over three years and also enjoy various volunteering.

My coding experience has included one semester of Computer Science in Java at the University of Puget Sound, as well as online coursework in Python and SQL.
